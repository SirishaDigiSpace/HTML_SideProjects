' Travel until you reach the destination of happiness, joy, memories....

Travel is not just about going or being transported from place to place ,but to explore the inner-self and inner-child in oneself. It is typically to explore, experience, or engage with different environments, cultures, and people. It encompasses a broad range of activities, including leisure tourism, business trips, educational journeys, and more. Travel can involve various modes of transportation, such as walking, cycling, driving, flying, or sailing. People give a fresh and strong comeback to their sedentary life after going to tour,trip or even a picnic to an enthusiastic place. Some travel to explore new places and journal the new , famous things, lifestyle, rituals, habits and routines that the citizens follow in different places. During this travel , new information, hidden facts, maybe the issues facing by the people come to limelight. Travel to some particular places will also introduce people to the adventures.

These are found in places like himalayas, mountain places, hill stations etc. Memories can be created while travel with friends , family , colleagues. The ancient arts, sculptures, other architecture is witnessed when we travel in person to the places like monuments rather than looking at images, photograph. At its core, travel offers individuals the opportunity to break away from their daily routines, broaden their perspectives, and create lasting memories. It allows people to immerse themselves in new landscapes, cuisines, languages, and customs, fostering personal growth and cultural understanding.

Travel may be local, regional, national(domestic) or international. While travel to different places safety measures also plays a vital role as there will be change in weather, lifestyle, food, clothing etc. Precautions are to be taken to avoid inconvenience during travel and while staying at place. From visiting iconic landmarks and natural wonders to engaging in adventurous activities and culinary experiences, travel presents endless possibilities for exploration and discovery. Whether embarking on a solo adventure, a family vacation, or a group tour, each journey holds the potential for meaningful encounters, self-reflection, and moments of awe and inspiration. Ultimately, travel enriches lives by providing opportunities for learning, relaxation, and connection with the world and those who inhabit it. It encourages individuals to step outside their comfort zones, embrace new challenges, and embrace the beauty of diversity, making it a cherished aspect of the human experience.

REQUIREMENTS...
PLANNING
Planning incudes dates, availability and willingness for all the members who are part of travel, accommodation at the destination, luggage with all the necessary items, other sources like food, travel tickets, travel resources such as source of travel(bus/train/flight/car.,etc..).

TIME
People choose to travel during weekends for a small trip/tour. For vacations, they choose to travel when they have more leisure period of time else an occasion like festivals, family functions and many more...Time is to be divided equally for shopping necessary items, packing luggage, checking availability and slots of tickets, if available - booking tickets, and to be prior to the arrival of the travel service(bus/train/flight etc.).There are times at when offer is available on tickets along with additional fancy programmes. Especially during local and globally celebrated events. People mostly tend to travel at this time.

ELIGIBILITY & GUIDELINES
Some places restrict visitors on basis of age group. Special permissions or requests can be proposed if necessary but only in rare cases. For foreign places, all customers are required to have the following applicable documents on hand on scheduled flights for entry: Passport. Visa, permanent residence card, temporary residence card, or valid visa exemption certificate.Passport will be approved after submitting required documents, so documents are to be ready and the whole process takes time. Similarly,for visa ,it may take more time as many applications are present. The permanent residence card or temporary residence card may or may not be required since the cause of travel varies from person to person.

The valid visa exemption certificate is to issued in order to clarify that visa is valid and approved legally without any fake documents or morphing. Fake documents or illegal approval of passport, visa, or any other will be punished and prosecuted. The people who are traveling also need to get a certificate from medical department such that if the person is healthy or has some health issues special care is provided. if not it is a risk or not advisable to travel. Financial capability is required because the cost of living of a place varies and people need to cope with the expenses and in case of robbery. The luggage carried by the individual should not contain harmful equipments with the mentality to harm others. Luggage can contain things that are necessary for safety. Proper food is to be carried such that while traveling some foods like processed foods are to be avoided.

EXPERIENCE...

' Travel memories are like footprints in the sand; they fade with time, but the imprints they leave on our hearts endure forever...'
Traveling is not just about reaching a destination; it's about the journey, the experiences, and the memories created along the way.This travel experience encounters some quotes that touches hearts and very connected to each and every travel enthusiastic individuals.

"The world is a book, and those who do not travel read only a page." - Saint Augustine.

Embarking on a journey through the rugged landscapes of Patagonia was like stepping into a dream. As our small group trekked along winding trails, surrounded by towering peaks and glistening glaciers, each moment felt like a painting come to life.

"To travel is to discover that everyone is wrong about other countries." - Aldous Huxley

Our days began with the soft hues of sunrise painting the sky, casting a golden glow over the jagged peaks. With backpacks laden and spirits high, we set off on our daily adventures, eager to explore the untamed wilderness that lay ahead.

"The journey not the arrival matters." - T.S. Eliot

Every step brought us closer to nature's wonders. We crossed icy rivers, navigated through dense forests, and marveled at the sheer magnitude of the towering mountains that stood sentinel over this pristine land. Each vista seemed more breathtaking than the last, leaving us in awe of the raw beauty that surrounded us.

"Travel is fatal to prejudice, bigotry, and narrow-mindedness." - Mark Twain

But it wasn't just the landscapes that captivated us; it was the people we met along the way. From sharing stories around crackling campfires to bonding over hearty meals cooked on makeshift stoves, we formed friendships that transcended language barriers and cultural differences. In those moments, we were united by our shared love for adventure and our mutual respect for the world around us.

"The real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes, but in having new eyes." - Marcel Proust

As the sun dipped below the horizon each evening, painting the sky in a tapestry of colors, we found ourselves filled with a profound sense of gratitude. Gratitude for the opportunity to explore this breathtaking corner of the world, gratitude for the friendships forged along the way, and gratitude for the simple joys of life on the road.

"A journey is best measured in friends, rather than miles." - Tim Cahill

And as our journey drew to a close, we carried with us not just memories, but a newfound appreciation for the beauty of our planet and the power of human connection. For in the end, it's not just the destinations that define a journey, but the experiences shared and the lessons learned along the way. "I am not the same, having seen the moon shine on the other side of the world." - Mary Anne Radmacher

These quotes beautifully encapsulate the transformative power and enriching experiences that come with travel. This travel experience captures the essence of exploration, connection, and appreciation for the natural world.
